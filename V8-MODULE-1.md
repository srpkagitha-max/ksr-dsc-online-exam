# V8 Module 1 — Students & Contacts

Included:
- Institute → Batch → Students drill-down
- Only selected batch students are shown
- Student phone and roll fields
- Active / On Hold / Inactive status
- Contact List built from submitted exam results
- Contact List search/filter and PDF/print
- Dashboard card renamed to Students & Contacts

Firebase rules do not need a new collection for this module. Existing `studentMaster` and `results` collections are used.
